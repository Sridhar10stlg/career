## Time budget (your real constraint)

- **Weekdays:** 2.5 hrs/day × 5 = 12.5 hrs
- **Weekends:** 8 hrs/day × 2 = 16 hrs
- **Total:** ~28.5 hrs/week ≈ **120 hrs/month ≈ 1,400 hrs/year**

That is a LOT if spent well, and almost nothing if spent scattered. The entire roadmap is built around protecting and focusing these hours. See [10 — Brutally Honest Assessment](10-brutally-honest-assessment.md) for how most people waste them.

## The 4-phase vision (your words, sequenced)

1. **Phase 1 (now → ~18 mo):** Strong hands-on Data Engineer + AI Engineer
2. **Phase 2 (~12 → 30 mo):** Solution Architect who designs enterprise systems
3. **Phase 3 (~24 mo+):** Technology Consultant — business fluency, stakeholder influence, architecture roadmaps
4. **Phase 4 (~30–42 mo):** Land a top-tier role (Senior DE / AI Engineer / Solutions Architect / Staff / Consultant)

These overlap on purpose. You do not finish one before starting the next.
